We Are CTRL

Chakan Testing and Research Lab is a state of the art Modern metal testing Laboratory situated in the Heart of Chakan Industrial hub of Pune, Maharashtra. The Lab is accredited as per ISO 17025 by NABL. Located at Chakan – Talegaon Road, Kharabwadi village of Chakan it is easily approachable from Pune & it suburban areas.The Lab was established in 2019 by a highly experienced and qualified person who has spent more than 4 decades in Premier Engineering Industries in India & abroad.

About The Company

Why Choose Us

Accredited Expertise

An ISO-17025 accredited lab by NABL ensures adherence to international standards, providing reliable and accurate testing results. Highly qualified and experienced personnel with over 4 decades of industry expertise ensure quality and precision in every test conducted.

Comprehensive Testing Services

Offering a wide range of testing facilities including Mechanical, Chemical, Metallurgical, and Corrosion tests, ensuring a one-stop solution for all testing requirements. Advanced testing methods and state-of-the-art equipment ensure thorough and comprehensive evaluations, covering various materials and components.

Customer-Centric Approach

Committed to customer satisfaction, offering quick turnaround times, cost-effective solutions, and maintaining confidentiality of test results. Expertise available for pinpointing failure reasons and suggesting remedial measures, providing valuable insights and recommendations to overcome challenges and ensure product reliability.

Proven Track Record & Reliability

Trusted by premier engineering industries in India & abroad, reflecting a proven track record of reliability, quality, and excellence in service. Transparent decision-making processes and adherence to established protocols and standards, ensuring trustworthiness and credibility in every testing procedure.
